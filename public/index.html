Whether you are looking for local domestic Window Cleaner or commercial Window Cleaning, we can help!

We predominately use the (Cold & Hot) water fed pole for most cleaning, safely from the ground.

This process uses 100% pure water, free from undissolved minerals and chemicals, both guaranteeing a long lasting and streak free Window Cleaning every time.

The benefits of using the water fed pole system is not only just safety for our staff but is also kind to the environment, and the soft bristled brushes and pure water are gentle and safe for your windows whether they are wooden, PVC or metal framed. The brushes can clean all parts of the glass and frames leaving you with a spot free finish and the water drains harmlessly away afterwards.

Our poles extend up to 50 feet which is usually sufficient to reach the highest buildings around the area. In certain circumstances we use cherry picking equipment, which enable us to give complete satisfaction. Our services are both one-off cleans as well as regular cleaning every 4 weeks.

We also offer a range of other external cleaning including gutter cleaning and clearing, fascias cleaning, conservatory cleaning, roof cleaning, solar panels, moss removal, Pressure washing and Soft washing of driveways, patios, paths, walls, decking and any other items or surface you feel could do with a real freshen up.
